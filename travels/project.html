    Project:-

    Break Bulk Operations

    Often Break Bulk Cargo is shipped using special equipment which can prove extremely expensive. With competent stevedoring partners located in key transport hubs around the world, Globe Trekkers is able to negotiate space on conventional vessels for break bulk, dramatically reducing costs whilst providing reliable cargo transportation.

    This break bulk operation requires determination and commitment, as well as solid knowledge of specific ports and shipping lines. Our endeavor is to look for the most effective and logical solution. Our team members have the rich experience and are well versed with the break bulk cargo handling.

    Key features of break bulk operations

    ✓ Cost effective and highly professional cargo handling

    ✓ Competitive pricing

    ✓ Efficient tally and supervision of cargo discharge / load

    ✓ Continuous management at every stage of the shipment

    ✓ Tie-downs and locked services

    ✓ Freight recovery services

    ✓Hazardous shipment handling

    ✓ Oversize / Overweight cargo handling

    ✓Charter services
